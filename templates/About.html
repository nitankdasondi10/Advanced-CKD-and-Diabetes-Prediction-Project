About Our Disease Prediction System

The following sections describe the types of diseases our app can help predict, along with important information about each condition.

Kidney Disease

Overview

The kidneys play vital roles in body function by:

  • Filtering blood and removing waste products
  • Balancing electrolyte levels in the body
  • Controlling blood pressure
  • Stimulating red blood cell production

The kidneys are located in the abdomen toward the back, normally one on each side of the spine. They receive blood through the renal arteries and return blood via the renal veins to the vena cava.

What is Chronic Kidney Disease?

Chronic kidney disease (CKD) means your kidneys are damaged and can't filter blood properly. The disease is called "chronic" because the damage happens gradually over a long period, causing wastes to build up in your body and potentially leading to other health problems.

Symptoms of Advanced CKD

  • Chest pain
  • Dry skin
  • Shortness of breath
  • Itching or numbness
  • Sleep problems
  • Headaches
  • Changes in urination
  • Trouble concentrating
  • Muscle cramps
  • Unexplained weight loss

Diabetes

Overview

Diabetes is a chronic condition that occurs when blood glucose (blood sugar) levels become too high. This happens when:

  • The body doesn't produce enough insulin (Type 1 Diabetes)
  • The body can't effectively use the insulin it produces (Type 2 Diabetes)

Over time, uncontrolled high blood glucose can lead to serious health complications. While diabetes has no cure, proper management can help maintain health and prevent complications.

Potential Complications

  • Increased risk of stroke
  • Kidney disease (nephropathy)
  • Eye problems (retinopathy)
  • Dental disease
  • Nerve damage (neuropathy)
  • Foot problems

Note: These predictions are based on statistical models and should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult with a healthcare provider for medical concerns.
